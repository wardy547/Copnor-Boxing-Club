We are an experienced boxing gym…..

Copnor boxing club has over 60 years experience between all of its coaches. We have trained 1000s of fighters throughout the years and quite a few have even gone professional.

But we want more people to learn this great sport, whether you'r big, small, old or young. We welcome all ages and sizes.

If you’re looking to get fit, or to become an amateur boxer. This is the perfect place for you. We have gloves and skipping ropes for you to use. All you have to do, is bring handwraps and turn up.

We are located in an old school boxing gym, behind the Astroturf at Moneyfields sport & social club in baffins, portsmouth. There is on site parking, which is free of charge.

We hold training sessions on Tuesdays and Thursdays at 7 pm till 9pm. Each session costs £3. Sessions are discounted to £2.50 if you are paying in bulk.
